How to Make a Hostel Booking

1

Create Your Account

Sign up for a student account using your university email address. Complete your profile with personal information and student ID verification.

2

Browse Available Rooms

Explore available hostel rooms by location, room type, and amenities. View room photos, prices, and availability for each semester.

3

Select Your Room

Choose your preferred room and select the semester period. Review the room details, pricing, and any additional requirements.

4

Make Payment

Proceed to payment using M-Pesa or cash payment options. Complete the payment process to confirm your booking.

5

Receive Confirmation

Once payment is verified, you'll receive a booking confirmation with your room assignment and check-in details.
